About FitFusion

FitFusion is an all-in-one fitness and nutrition app that provides structured workout programs, recipe ideas, and simple meal guidance. It is designed to help users build muscle, lose fat, and improve performance with clear, no-nonsense training and nutrition support.

The app is built and maintained by an experienced strength & conditioning coach and educator with years of experience working with athletes and general population clients.

Getting Started

Create an Account

Download FitFusion from the App Store and create an account using your email or Apple sign-in (depending on app options). Once logged in, you can browse programs, recipes, and training content.

Accessing Programs

Some content may be available for free, while full access requires a subscription. After you purchase a subscription through the App Store, your premium content should unlock automatically when you reopen the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. I paid but my subscription didn’t unlock.

First, close and reopen the app. Make sure you are logged in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase the subscription. If content is still locked, contact support@fitfusion.health with a copy of your App Store receipt so we can help.

2. How do I cancel my subscription?

All billing is handled by Apple. To manage or cancel:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad.
  • Tap your name at the top.
  • Tap Subscriptions.
  • Select FitFusion and choose the option to manage or cancel.

3. Does FitFusion provide medical or dietician advice?

No. FitFusion provides general fitness and nutrition guidance only and is not a substitute for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a physician or qualified health professional before starting a new diet or exercise program.

4. The app is crashing or not loading correctly.

Try these steps:

  • Close and reopen the app.
  • Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed from the App Store.
  • Restart your device.

If the issue continues, email support with details and screenshots so we can investigate.

Privacy & Data

FitFusion respects your privacy. The app may store basic information such as your preferences, workout selections, and progress to personalize your experience.

Payment information is never handled directly by FitFusion. All purchases, subscriptions, and billing are processed securely by Apple through the App Store.

FitFusion does not sell your personal information. Any analytics or usage data is used solely to improve app performance and features.

If you have questions or requests related to your data, please contact support@fitfusion.health.
